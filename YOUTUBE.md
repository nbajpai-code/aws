# Top AWS YouTube Channels & Courses (2025)

## 📺 Best Channels for AWS Certs
*   **[Stephane Maarek](https://www.youtube.com/@StephaneMaarek)**: The most popular AWS instructor. His YouTube has summaries, but his full courses (Udemy) are the gold standard.
*   **[AWS (Official)](https://www.youtube.com/@amazonwebservices)**: "This is My Architecture" series is fantastic for the Professional level.
*   **[Be A Better Dev (Garrick)](https://www.youtube.com/@BeABetterDev)**: Deep dives into serverless, DynamoDB, and real-world patterns.
*   **[Digital Cloud Training (Neal Davis)](https://www.youtube.com/@DigitalCloudTraining)**: Excellent exam walkthroughs and cheat sheets.
*   **[freeCodeCamp](https://www.youtube.com/@freecodecamp)**: Hosts full 10+ hour certification courses from Andrew Brown (ExamPro).

## 🎓 Recommended Videos
1.  **[AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner - Full Course](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SXOJ6t7e7Ms)** (freeCodeCamp): A complete free course to get you certified.
2.  **[AWS re:Invent Keynotes](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=aws+reinvent+keynote)**: Watch these to stay updated on the latest services (GenAI, Q, Bedrock).
