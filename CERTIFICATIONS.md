# AWS Certification Tracks (2025)

## 🏗️ Foundational
*   **[AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-cloud-practitioner/)**: (CLF-C02) The starting point. Cloud fluency.
*   **[AWS Certified AI Practitioner](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-ai-practitioner/)**: (AIF-C01) **NEW** for 2025. Validates understanding of AI/ML/GenAI models.

## 🧱 Associate Level
*   **[Solutions Architect – Associate](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-solutions-architect-associate/)**: (SAA-C03) The most popular cloud cert. Focuses on resilient and cost-optimized design.
*   **[Developer – Associate](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-developer-associate/)**: (DVA-C02) For writing and deploying apps on AWS.
*   **[Data Engineer – Associate](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-data-engineer-associate/)**: (DEA-C01) core data-related AWS services.
*   **[Machine Learning Engineer - Associate](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-machine-learning-engineer-associate/)**: (MLA-C01) **NEW**. Implementation of ML workloads.

## 🏛️ Professional Level
*   **[Solutions Architect – Professional](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-solutions-architect-professional/)**: (SAP-C02) Advanced design and complex distributed systems.
*   **[DevOps Engineer – Professional](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-devops-engineer-professional/)**: (DOP-C02) Provisioning, operating, and managing distributed systems.

## 🔬 Specialty
*   **[Advanced Networking - Specialty](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-advanced-networking-specialty/)**: (ANS-C01) Complex networking tasks (Direct Connect, Transit Gateway).
*   **[Security - Specialty](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-security-specialty/)**: (SCS-C02) Securing data and workloads.
